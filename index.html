Mathemagics

About

Mathemagics is a game about making equations out numbers and operations to fight. This paper prototype demonstrates the battle system.

How To Play

Materials Required

In order to play our prototype Mathemagics, you'll need to have some cards and stuff :D make sure you have THESE

  1. Two people
  2. Deck of 20 Number cards

    You'll need a deck of 20 cards, containing two of each number between 0 and 9.

  3. Deck of 10 Operation cards

    You'll need a deck of 10 operation cards, containing four addition cards, four subtraction cards, and two multiplication cards.

  4. Set of dice: at least 1 six sided dice and 1 four sided dice

    5. It is possible to play without a four sided dice as long as you reroll on 5s and 6s on a standard dice.

  5. Index Cards

Setting Up

One person will be the Player, one person will be the Enemy. The Enemy is in charge of shuffling and distributing cards, and overall running the game.

Playing

The Player goes first. Shuffle the operator and number decks separately.

Winning

The goal is to get your enemy to 0 HP to win! If the player drops to 0 HP before their enemy, they lose.
